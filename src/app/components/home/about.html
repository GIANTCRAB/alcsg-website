A Little About Us

ALittleChange, is an organisation that started off by like-minded people with similar aspiration of making our society a better one. As the years goes by, increasing number of youth are beginning to take interest in volunteering and the more important thing would be to educate the youth the significance of volunteering so that they can understand the needs of the society. We aim to be a catalyst, a platform to improve the situation in today's society where youth can inculcate volunteering into their lives and help the ones in need.
