A Little About Us

ALittleChange is a youth-run, nonprofit organisation that works to strengthen family relationships in a low-income setting, while engaging the youth community in Singapore.

Here, we value conversation and interaction, and are always on the lookout for new stories to listen to, and new ways we can engage the people we work with.

It is our hope that through small acts of kindness, we will make big differences in peoples lives.

Vision

ALittleChange aims to create sustainable avenues that strengthen family relationships in a low-income setting while engaging the Singaporean youth community in volunteerism.

Mission

  • To take a ground-up approach to problem solving, and to work with families within their individual situational contexts
  • To inform and educate youths on income inequality in Singapore
  • To be a platform that youths can use to learn how to, or start their initiatives.
